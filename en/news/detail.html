Hongyixin Deepens Strategic Partnership with International Brand

Recently, Shenzhen Hongyixin Technology Co., Ltd. signed a strategic cooperation supplement with a leading international component manufacturer. The two sides will deepen cooperation in authorized distribution, technical support and market promotion for passive components and power management products.

Under the agreement, Hongyixin will expand the authorized product lines and strengthen warehousing and logistics in South and East China to better serve local customers. The parties will also hold joint technical seminars and customer training to support product innovation and quality.

Hongyixin stated that this partnership upgrade continues the company’s “Professional, Efficient, Trustworthy” approach, and that it will continue to focus on customer needs and provide quality supply chain and solutions support.
