Company Profile

Shenzhen Hongyixin Technology Co., Ltd. is an authorized distributor focused on electronic component distribution and technical solutions. Based in Shenzhen, we serve customers across China and overseas, providing quality passive and active components and one-stop support from selection and sampling to volume production.

We maintain long-term authorized partnerships with leading international manufacturers. Our product lines cover capacitors, resistors, inductors, ICs, diodes, transistors, connectors and more. With a dedicated technical team and robust supply chain, we respond quickly to customer needs and support product innovation.

Our Mission

To provide reliable component supply and technical support with professionalism and integrity, and to be a trusted partner to our customers.

Our Vision

To be a leading company in component distribution and solutions, creating value and growing together with customers.
